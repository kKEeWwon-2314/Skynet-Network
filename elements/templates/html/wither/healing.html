Healing

"You may be dead, but I am still healing"- Scarf


A Withermancer (you) needs to know how to heal effectively.

Healing can be achieved through Withermancy itself, but sometimes that isn't enough.


One way to heal yourself is through literature. Reading lexical context is a great way to heal as it allows you to

be immersed in another world. After a while, you might shimmer to realize that your are back from the dead.

There are also a arrangement of healing abilities, which certain you should look at.


Here is a list of healing abilties, only to name a few:

  • Giga Heal (Ability)
  • Relic of Atonement
  • Absorption Shield
  • Critical Wither Shield (Ability)
  • Extreme Measures (Ability)
  • Overflow Conversions
  • Blood of the Superior *

* The Blood of the Superior is a very special healing ability that is outdated, so you can ignore it.
